MARCO ULPIO TRAJANO
Marco Ulpio Trajano was the first emperor of Rome who wasn't born in Rome or even in the italic peninsula, he was born in Hispania. He is known as one of the 5 good emperors and he probably was the best emperor of Rome.
The other 4 good emperors
Marco Coceyo Nerva | 96-98
Publio Elio Adriano | 117-138
Antonino Pio | 138-161
Marco Aurelio | 161-180
